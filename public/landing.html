Our Features

Everything You Need

A complete suite of tools to manage your retail inventory — from purchase orders to sales analytics.

Real-time Stock Tracking

AFTER UPDATE triggers keep inventory in sync automatically. Stock levels update the moment a transaction happens.

Multi-Warehouse Management

Track and transfer stock across multiple warehouse locations with individual capacity and manager records.

Analytics Dashboard

Revenue trends, top-selling products, stock valuation, and inventory turnover with interactive charts.

Purchase Order Workflow

Full PO lifecycle: Draft → Approved → Ordered → Received → Closed. Auto-update stock on receipt.

Sales Order Processing

End-to-end sales: create orders, reserve inventory, track payments, and manage fulfillment.

Role-Based Access Control

JWT authentication with Admin, Manager, Staff, and Viewer roles with granular permission checks.

Low Stock Alerts

Automatic notifications when products drop below reorder levels. Never miss a restock.

Audit Trail

Every data modification logged with before/after snapshots for full compliance traceability.

Reports & ABC Analysis

Inventory valuation, sales summaries, purchase reports, and ABC classification.
